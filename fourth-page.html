Fall 2024

September: A short two-day trip to San Diego

This September, I traveled to San Diego with my friends. On the first day, we visited the Safari Zoo Park, where we saw a wide variety kinds of animals, such as giraffes and tigers. We took a guided tour on a safari truck that allowed us to get closer with wild herbivores. Observing these aninals made me realize the importance of protecting our planet and the species we share it with. Overall, it was a really fun experience.

On Day 2, we visited the beach in downtown San Diego. The view was absolutely stunning. I used my film camera to capture many beatiful moments of the natural scenery and the time with my friends. Feel free to explore the photo gallery to see the highlights of my SD trip.
