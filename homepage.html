About my Final Project

My name is Angelina Lin. For my ITP 104 Final Project, I decided to make a 2024 photo gallery of my life. I decided to apply the web development skills I have learned throughout this semester to build a web-based photo gallery that would not only train and strength my web-building skills, but also reflect and cherish my memeories. As the year comes to a close, I hope that looking back on this website in the future will bring back all of my pleasant memories. I also welcome and invite everyone to take a glance at my photo gallery and share in the delight I have felt.
